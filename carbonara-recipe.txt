Ingredients:
	- 2 eggs
	- bacon cubes
	- grana
	- black pepper
	- spaghetti

Directions:
	1. Put the spaghetti on salted boiling water.
	2. Cook the bacon in a small pan.
	3. Mix the two eggs for a minute until you get yellow-like liquid.
	4. When the spaghetti is ready, take out the water and put the spaghetti back on the hot pot, now without water.
	5. Put all of the other ingredients in the pot with the spaghetti.
	6. Mix until all the ingredients are uniformly distributed.
	7. Serve hot.
